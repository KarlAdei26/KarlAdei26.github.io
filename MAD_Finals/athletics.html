Athletics

Focus on improving your speed, endurance, and technique with these practical steps.

Steps to Improve
1. Warm up properly before training to prevent injuries.
2. Incorporate interval sprints and long-distance runs.
3. Add strength training: squats, lunges, core exercises.
4. Track your times and gradually increase intensity.
5. Include recovery days and monitor your nutrition.
6. Focus on running form and breathing techniques for efficiency.
Daily Tips
• Keep a training log to monitor progress.
• Stay hydrated before, during, and after workouts.
• Mix up workouts to prevent plateaus and keep it fun.
• Practice mindfulness to improve focus and performance.
Common Mistakes
• Skipping warm-ups or cool-downs.
• Overtraining without rest.
• Ignoring proper running form.
• Poor nutrition affecting energy and recovery.
Motivation
“Consistency beats intensity. Small, steady improvements lead to big results over time.”
