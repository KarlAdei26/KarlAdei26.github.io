Cycling

Boost your cycling skills, speed, and endurance with these focused steps.

Steps to Improve
1. Warm up properly with easy pedaling and stretches.
2. Practice pedaling technique for efficiency and cadence.
3. Include interval sprints to increase power.
4. Work on bike handling: cornering, braking, and balance.
5. Strengthen legs and core off the bike with exercises.
6. Track your rides and gradually increase distance and intensity.
Daily Tips
• Focus on smooth pedal strokes and proper posture.
• Practice short bursts of speed regularly.
• Include stretching and foam rolling for recovery.
• Track performance to see gradual improvement.
Common Mistakes
• Poor pedaling technique reducing efficiency.
• Ignoring bike handling skills.
• Overtraining without proper recovery.
• Neglecting off-bike strength and core exercises.
Motivation
“Strong technique and consistent practice make every ride better.”
