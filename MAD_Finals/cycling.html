Steps to Improve
1. Warm up with easy pedaling.
2. Practice smooth cadence.
3. Add interval sprints.
4. Train hills and climbs.
5. Strengthen legs and core.
6. Track progress weekly.
Daily Tips
• Maintain proper posture on the bike.
• Stretch after every ride.
• Stay hydrated.
• Ride consistently, not just hard.
Mindset
“Smooth effort builds lasting endurance.”
