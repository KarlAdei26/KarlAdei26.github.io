Steps to Improve
1. Warm up with easy laps.
2. Practice proper breathing.
3. Train all strokes.
4. Include interval sets.
5. Strengthen core and shoulders.
6. Track lap times.
Daily Tips
• Focus on smooth strokes.
• Stretch shoulders after swimming.
• Stay consistent with practice.
• Rest between hard sessions.
Mindset
“Every lap builds discipline and control.”
