Swimming

Enhance your strength, speed, and technique in the water with these tips.

Steps to Improve
1. Warm up with dynamic stretches and short swims.
2. Practice all strokes: freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly.
3. Include interval training for speed and endurance.
4. Strengthen core and shoulders with dry-land exercises.
5. Focus on breathing technique and efficient stroke form.
6. Track lap times and gradually increase training intensity.
Daily Tips
• Keep a swimming log to monitor progress.
• Maintain proper hydration before and after practice.
• Vary drills to prevent boredom and improve technique.
• Include recovery days for muscles to repair and grow.
Common Mistakes
• Skipping warm-ups or cooldowns.
• Poor breathing technique affecting endurance.
• Neglecting strength training.
• Overtraining without rest.
Motivation
“Stroke by stroke, consistency builds champions.”
